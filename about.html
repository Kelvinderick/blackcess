About Blackcess

Nigeria's premium airline, connecting West Africa to the world with safety, comfort, and reliability at our core.

Our Story

Born in Nigeria.
Built for the World.

Blackcess Airlines was founded with a single, clear purpose — to give Nigerians access to world-class air travel without compromise. We believe every passenger deserves punctual flights, attentive service, and the dignity of a truly premium experience, whether flying Lagos to Abuja or Abuja to London.

From our first domestic sector to our flagship transatlantic route, we have grown steadily by earning the trust of every passenger who steps aboard. We are not just an airline. We are Nigeria's statement to the world.

Mission & Vision

Why We Fly

Our Mission is to provide safe, comfortable, and reliable air travel that connects people and creates opportunity — for our passengers, our employees, and the communities we serve.

Our Vision is to be West Africa's most trusted airline, recognised globally for operational excellence, customer experience, and the pride we bring to Nigerian aviation.

Our Promise — every Blackcess flight departs on time, arrives safely, and treats every passenger with the respect they deserve. That is not a marketing line. It is our operating standard.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Safety First

Safety is non-negotiable at Blackcess. Every aircraft, every crew member, and every procedure is held to the highest international standard.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in everything — from the cleanliness of our cabins to the responsiveness of our call centre.

Integrity

We are honest with our passengers, our partners, and ourselves. No hidden fees. No false promises. Just straightforward service.

People First

Our passengers and our people are at the heart of every decision we make. A great airline is built on great people.

Nigerian Pride

We are proudly Nigerian. Everything we do is a reflection of the potential, ambition, and excellence of this nation.

Reliability

Our passengers make plans around our schedules. We take that responsibility seriously and work tirelessly to honour it.

Milestones

Our Journey So Far

Founded
Blackcess Airlines Incorporated
Blackcess was incorporated as a Nigerian airline with a mandate to redefine domestic aviation standards.
First Flight
Lagos–Abuja Domestic Service Launched
Our inaugural flight connected Lagos (LOS) and Abuja (ABV), marking the beginning of a new chapter in Nigerian aviation.
Expansion
Port Harcourt & Kano Added
Domestic network expanded to cover Port Harcourt (PHC), Kano (KAN), and Benin City (BNI), bringing more Nigerians into our network.
Regional
West Africa Routes — Accra & Dakar
Blackcess crossed borders, launching regional routes to Accra (ACC) and Dakar (DSS), connecting West Africa.
International
Abuja–London Heathrow & Gatwick
Our flagship international route launched — daily service from Abuja (ABV) to London Heathrow (LHR) and Gatwick (LGW), putting Blackcess on the global map.
2026
Continued Growth
Blackcess continues to grow its network, fleet, and team — with more destinations and partnerships on the horizon.
