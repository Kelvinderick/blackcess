Pay Small Small lets you confirm your seat with a deposit, then clear the balance on a fixed schedule before departure. Available on selected Economy fares to LHR/LGW and key domestic routes.

How Pay Small Small works

  1. Book & pay deposit

    Reserve with 10% (or higher on promo seats). Your PNR is held not ticketed yet.

  2. Instalment schedule

    Blackcess sends due dates by SMS. Missed payments may cancel the booking per fare rules.

  3. Full payment & ticket

    E-ticket emailed once balance is zero. Travel only on the issued ticket.

Admin fee may apply. Credit check required on first use. Not combinable with all discount fares.
