TRAVEL ADVISORY: TRANSIT PROCEDURES FOR LONDON-BOUND PASSENGERS VIA ABUJA (ABV)

Passengers travelling through Abuja (ABV) and connecting to Blackcess's London flights (Heathrow or Gatwick) are hereby advised to collect their checked baggage upon arrival in Abuja and re-check in for their onward international flight. This procedure is mandatory for all transiting passengers.

Please note that failure to retrieve and re-check in your baggage may result in delays or missed connections.

To ensure a smooth travel experience, passengers are strongly encouraged to allow adequate connection time in Abuja for:

  • Baggage collection
  • Security screening
  • International check-in formalities

We kindly request all passengers, travel agencies, and corporate partners to take note of this important travel information.
